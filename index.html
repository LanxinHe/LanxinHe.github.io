In the beginning

Posted on 2022-07-09 Edited on 2022-07-19

Yesterday I managed to establish my first website following the online construction. Clearly, there are many items that I am not quite familiar with, but the whole process seems go well.

I always think my life is full of isolation, tedious routine, and many things that go against my expectation. However, I do meet some lovely people along my life journey, and I am grateful for their understanding and support, especially when I was buried in my negative feelings and behaved childishly.

By building this personal website, I am actually constructing a space which has nothing to do with social, and a room to store my thoughts, to record my learning. I wish after many years when I look back, what is left is just the gratitude for everything unfolding in my life.

Also, even my English is far from that of a native speaker, I will try to use it to express my feelings since this is included in the attention I build this website. But I will still use my mother tongue in some cases.

I will share almost everything in my life in this blog, but most would be my learning record. I am currently studying wireless communication, focusing on the massive input massive output (MIMO) signal processing. Hence many of my blogs would to do with this area, but I am also interested in machine learning. Therefore the wireless communication and machine learning would be two main branches of my record. Besides, I am now in a really serious relationship, I wish this blog can also record those sweet memories and goes as far as our relationship.
